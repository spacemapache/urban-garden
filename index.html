About Royal Pickering

Designed by world-renowned architects WOHA, PARKROYAL COLLECTION Pickering is an iconic eco-friendly hotel in Singapore, with a stunning hotel-in-a-garden concept that incorporates energy-saving features and seamlessly blends cutting-edge environmentally-friendly practices and technologies throughout the building, Awarded 'World's Leading Green City Hotel' in the prestigious World Travel Awards, the hotel positions Singapore as a world leader of green high-rise developments.

  • First hotel-in-a-garden in Singapore's business district
  • 4 landscaped terraces with distinct natural environments, inspired by rice paddies.
  • Designed by WOHA, a Singapore-based architectural practice famous for biophillic designs.

Urban Farm

The rooftop Urban Farm provides fresh ingredients for farm-to-table dining, which reduces hotel's reliance on food deliveries and lowers carbon footprint. It is home to over 50 varieties of vegetables, fruits, herbs and edible flowers. The Urban Farm provides carbon-neutral, eco-friendly garnishes, aromatics and ingredients to Pickering Hotel's restaurants and spas.
